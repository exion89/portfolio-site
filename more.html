My personal life

I love to travel.

Ever since I started watching Anthony Bourdain travel the world, trying exotic cuisine and making friends all over the planet, I've been addicted to traveling.

Once I finally had enough money to leave the US, I first went to Italy in 2015. I stayed in cheap hostels and had tight budgets, but I immediately fell in love with traveling and my addiction soon continued...

Very soon after returning from Italy, I decided to go to Thailand. It was an unforgettable experience, and I can't wait to go back to South East Asia. I love all food from every culture on the planet. My favorite food is probably Thai food, however there's pretty much nothing I won't eat. The US has the best food in the world because it's so diverse, and food is available from basically every country and people on the planet.

A few countries later and I decided that I wanted to live outside of the US. Politically and socially, I had so many issues with trying to start a family America. I decided to start dating in Mexico and eventually in 2018 fell in love with my wife, Veronica. On October 22, 2022 we welcomed a baby girl named Renata Madison.
